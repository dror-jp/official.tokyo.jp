## Disclaimer

- This site is **not a government website** and has no affiliation with the Tokyo
  Metropolitan Government, the ward offices, or any public body.
- This site provides **navigation, not legal advice**. Procedures, requirements and
  opening hours can change at any time. **Always confirm with the official source** —
  if anything on this site differs from an official page, the official page is correct.
- We are not responsible for the content of external websites, including the official
  websites we link to.
- "Last verified" means a human checked that the link worked and the information matched
  the official source **on that date** — not that it is still correct today.

## Link policy

We link only to:

1. **Official government domains** — city and ward sites (lg.jp), national ministries
   and agencies (go.jp), and metro.tokyo.lg.jp. These are always preferred.
2. **Public institutions** — official English pages of quasi-governmental bodies
   (for example, the Tokyo Intercultural Portal Site run by the Tokyo Metropolitan
   Foundation TSUNAGARI).
3. **Essential service providers** — for utilities only (electricity, gas), where the
   provider is a private company and no government page can start the service for you.

We never link to aggregator sites, affiliate sites, or advertising-supported guides.
Every link is verified by a human before it is published; anything we could not verify
is not published.
